Renaissance
Italienne

The High Renaissance is an artistic period of the Cinquecento which is located in the continuity of the First Renaissance.

It refers to the art of papal Rome, Florence and the Republic of Venice from 1500 to 1530. With the painting of the High Renaissance, some consider that Western art reached its peak and brings works of art of universal significance.
